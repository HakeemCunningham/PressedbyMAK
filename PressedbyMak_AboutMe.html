About Me

Hi, I'm Mak

I'm Makena Purvis, a 23-year-old woman born and raised in the picturesque city of Anchorage, Alaska. As an artist with a passion for creativity, I have carved a niche for myself in the world of beauty and fashion through my online nail business. Specializing in acrylic nail painting, I am thrilled to share my unique style and talent with clients all over the world.


I find joy in infusing vibrant colors and intricate designs into my acrylic nail creations. With meticulous attention to detail and a keen eye for aesthetics, I transform ordinary nails into stunning works of art. My innovative techniques and imaginative designs have garnered a loyal following, as clients appreciate the way I use their nails as a canvas for self-expression and individuality.


Through the power of the online platform, I have been able to reach a wider audience and expand my artistic influence far beyond the boundaries of Anchorage. It's my mission to empower individuals and instill confidence through my captivating nail art. With boundless enthusiasm and unwavering dedication to my craft, I am constantly pushing the boundaries of the nail art industry, one brushstroke at a time.
